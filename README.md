# Chocolate-Shipments-Dashboard
1. Project Title / Headline
ChocoTrack: Chocolate Shipments & Profitability Dashboard
An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze chocolate shipment performance, profitability, product demand, and regional trends across multiple countries.

2. Short Description / Purpose
This dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of chocolate shipment operations by tracking revenue, shipment volume, profit margins, product performance, and geographic distribution.
It helps business users monitor trends over time, identify top-performing products and salespersons, and optimize logistics and sales strategies.

6. Business Problem
Chocolate businesses often struggle to track shipment efficiency, product profitability, and regional performance using raw data alone.
Without a centralized analytics view, identifying trends, inefficiencies, and growth opportunities becomes difficult.

7. Business Impact & Insights
Sales Optimization: Identifies top-performing products and salespersons
Profit Analysis: Highlights high-margin and low-margin products
Regional Strategy: Helps focus on high-revenue geographies
Operational Planning: Supports better shipment and inventory decisions
Trend Analysis: Enables year-over-year performance comparison
